about LQAA

Professional licensing expertise and quality-assured training

LQAA Ltd is your trusted partner for all licensing and training requirements. As an NCFE and Highfield approved centre, we provide comprehensive solutions for businesses across West London, ensuring full compliance with all regulatory requirements while minimizing hassle and maximizing success rates.

Our experienced team handles everything from initial consultation through final approval. Whether you need a premises licence for your new restaurant, personal licence training for your staff, or comprehensive health and safety courses, LQAA delivers professional results every time.
